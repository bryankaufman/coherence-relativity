## 4. THE Пғ вҶ’ 0 LIMIT

### 4.1 Asymptotic Analysis

More precisely: For fixed t > 0, $\exp(-\arcsin(t)/(2Пғ)) вҶ’ 0$ as Пғ вҶ’ 0вҒә.

**More careful analysis**: The denominator receives contributions from all t вҲҲ (-1, 1), but as Пғ вҶ’ 0, the weight shifts toward t вҶ’ 0вҒ». The precise limit requires Laplace-type asymptotics.
