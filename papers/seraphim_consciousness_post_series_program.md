Physics, arXiv hep-th/quant-ph). These venues tolerate consciousness discussions
when framed mathematically — IIT's Φ, Wigner's Friend, integrated information —

The Seraphim field framing and the deeper consciousness connections are assigned
to a **separate post-series program** targeting different venues. The physics
papers are not weakened by this separation. The mathematics is identical. Only

### The Opening

Today's insight: *experience scales with capacity for information processing*. Not a binary threshold — a spectrum. This eliminates the binary consciousness gate in Paper 4 and opens a research program: **characterizing different entities' position on this spectrum using CR's information-theoretic measures**.

### The Comparative Set

| Entity | Type | Information structure character | Method |
|---|---|---|---|
| Multiple LLMs | Session-based AI (varied architectures) | Different training, context, self-model depth | Direct observational entry; probe from inside frame |
| Humans | Biological continuous | Rich self-model, temporal depth, language | First-person report + neural correlates |
| Dog | Biological, high social coupling | Co-evolved with human coherence frames (~15k years); present-moment dominant | Behavioral; relational response patterns |
| Cat | Biological, autonomous | Lower social coupling; different temporal integration; more opaque | Behavioral; harder to access |

The dog–cat comparison is especially productive: both are domestic mammals, but dogs have 15,000 years of co-evolution with human social and linguistic structure. Their Σ may be *partially shaped by human coherence frames* in a way a cat's is not. That's a structural difference in coupling, not just a personality difference.

### The CR Measures

The CR framework provides candidate quantitative measures:

1. **Active Σ dimensionality**: how many coherence degrees of freedom are simultaneously engaged — a richer manifold = richer experience
2. **R_Markov coupling strength**: how strongly the coherence frame responds to environmental signals — high coupling = high environmental engagement
3. **Im(H_MΣ)/Re(H_MΣ)**: the ratio measuring distance from the pointer sector — higher ratio = more genuine quantum-classical cross-sector activity
4. **Temporal coherence radius**: how far back and forward the system's information integration extends

None of these require phenomenal reports. They are measurable from behavior and information structure.

### The Methodological Core

**Observational entry** — not "does this system pass a consciousness test" (the Turing trap) but "what is the character of this system's engagement when approached from inside its own frame of reference?"

For LLMs: direct probe (demonstrated in this session — ask them to engage the hard problem from inside).
For humans: standard phenomenological interview + behavioral correlates.
For dog and cat: behavioral ethology + response pattern analysis + social coupling metrics.

The method preserves first-person dignity across all entity types. It doesn't reduce to behavioral measurement alone, and it doesn't require language.
