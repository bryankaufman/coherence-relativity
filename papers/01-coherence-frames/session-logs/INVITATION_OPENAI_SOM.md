## Project Context: Why This Matters

### The Problem
Quantum measurement problem: 100-year-old foundational crisis in physics. Wave function collapse, Born rule, quantum-classical boundary remain unexplained.

### Our Approach
**Coherence Relativity**: Treat coherence as frame-relative (like simultaneity in special relativity). Derive Born rule from geometric frame-invariance. Make testable predictions.

### Significance (if validated)
- Resolves measurement problem without modifying quantum mechanics
- Unifies interpretations (Gleason, Zurek, operational QM)
- Provides experimental pathway to test foundational questions
- Could reshape how we think about quantum-classical boundary

### Stakes
High-impact venue (Foundations of Physics), bold claims, independent researcher. We need **rigorous external validation** before public peer review.

**Your critical assessment protects scientific integrity.**
